Alders

Alder is the common name of a genus of flowering plants Alnus
belonging to the birch family Betulaceae. The genus comprises about
30 species of monoecious trees an shrubs, a few reaching a large size,
distributed throughout the north temperate zone with a few
species extending into Central America and the northern Andes.
